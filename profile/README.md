<h1>Our Products</h1>

<div>
  <h2>
    <a href="https://github.com/LanRhyme/MicYou">MicYou</a> <- Click to Visit
  </h2>
  A powerful tool that turns your Android device into a high-quality microphone for your PC. 
      
  <hr>
  
  <h3>Features</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Multiple Connection Modes</strong>: Support for Wi-Fi, USB (ADB/AOA), and Bluetooth.</li>
    <li><strong>Audio Processing</strong>:Built-in Noise Suppression, Auto Gain Control (AGC), and Dereverberation.</li>
    <li><strong>Cross-Platform</strong>:</li>
      <ul>
        <li>Android Client<strong></strong>: Modern Material 3 interface, dark/light theme support.</li>
        <li><strong>Desktop Server</strong>: Receive audio on Windows/Linux/macOS.</li>
      </ul>
    <li><strong>Virtual Microphone</strong>: Works seamlessly with VB-Cable to act as a system microphone input.</li>
    <li><strong>Customizable</strong>: Adjust sample rate, channel count, and audio format.</li>
  </ul>
